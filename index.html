About BINI

BINI is a talented P-pop girl group known for their empowering music and vibrant performances. In late 2023, they released the singles "Karera" and "Pantropiko," which gained significant recognition, particularly "Karera," which became popular at graduation ceremonies, and "Pantropiko," which went viral on TikTok, leading to a dance challenge that millions participated in. Their debut EP, *Talaarawan* (Diary), released on March 8, 2024, features these hits and emphasizes women's empowerment. BINI achieved remarkable milestones, including reaching over two million monthly listeners on Spotify, becoming the highest-streamed female OPM artist. They made history as the first Filipino artist to top Spotify's Daily Top Artists Philippines chart. BINI also participated in international events, showcasing their talent globally. Their commitment to creativity and community engagement has solidified their place as influential figures in the music industry.
